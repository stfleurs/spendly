What is Receet Pro?

Most budgeting apps show you where your money went after you've already spent it. Receet Pro works differently — it helps you decide what to spend before you spend, using the proven envelope method.

Think of it like cash in physical envelopes. When you get paid, you divide your money into labeled envelopes: Groceries, Gas, Dining Out, Fun Money, Savings, and so on. Each envelope holds what you've allocated for that category.

When you spend, the money comes out of the right envelope automatically. When an envelope is empty, you stop spending in that category. No guilt, no second-guessing, no end-of-month surprises — because you decided upfront what mattered.

Receet Pro digitizes this system and supercharges it with receipt scanning (OCR), real-time insights, multi-currency support, automatic categorization, and beautiful charts. Built with Flutter and Firebase, it's available on iOS, Android, macOS, Windows, and Linux.

How Receet Pro Works

Stop guessing. Start knowing. Four simple steps to financial clarity.

Add Your Income

Enter your salary, freelance earnings, side hustles — any source of income. Receet Pro works with multiple income streams and supports bi-weekly, semi-monthly, and monthly pay schedules.

Create Envelopes

Set up spending categories that match your life. Groceries, gas, dining, entertainment, shopping, savings — name them what you want and assign a monthly budget to each one.

Spend & Track

Snap a receipt and the OCR engine reads the total and categorizes it automatically. Or enter transactions manually. Each purchase deducts from the right envelope in real time.

Always Know Your Balance

Check any envelope balance in one tap. Green means you're on track. Yellow means you're getting low. Red means the envelope is empty. No more anxiety about whether you can afford something.

Everything you need to stay on top of your money

Built for people who want real answers, not complicated spreadsheets. Here's what Receet Pro does out of the box.

📨

Envelope Budgeting

Money is divided into digital envelopes that you define. When an envelope runs out, the spending in that category stops — by your own rule. Envelopes roll over or reset each month, your choice. This is the core mechanic that makes the system work: finite resources force intentional decisions.

📷

Receipt Scanning

Snap a photo of any receipt and Google ML Kit reads the merchant, date, and total. The transaction is categorized into the correct envelope automatically. No manual data entry, no remembering what you spent three days ago. The receipt image is stored for future reference.

📊

Insights & Reports

Interactive charts show spending trends across categories, months, and accounts. Compare this month to last month. Track net worth over time. See which categories consistently go over budget. Export everything to CSV or PDF for tax preparation or financial planning.

💳

Multi-Account Support

Link checking accounts, savings accounts, credit cards, and cash. See all balances in one dashboard. Track net worth with daily snapshots. Handle transfers between accounts seamlessly. Supports USD, EUR, HTG, and CAD with real-time exchange rates.

🔄

Recurring Bills

Set up bills and subscriptions that repeat weekly, monthly, or yearly. Get push notifications before payments are due. Track how much of your monthly income is already committed to recurring expenses. Never miss a payment or get hit with an overdraft fee again.

📁

Import & Export

Import transactions from CSV files or PDF bank statements. The parser handles multiple formats and maps them to your envelopes. Export your complete transaction history anytime — CSV for spreadsheets, PDF for records. Your data is yours to keep and transfer.

🔒

Security & Privacy

Optional PIN or biometric lock (Face ID / fingerprint) protects the app from unauthorized access. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using Firebase's infrastructure. Google Sign-In for convenient authentication. No third-party tracking or data selling.

🌐

Localization

Available in English, French, and Haitian Creole. Currency formatting adapts to your locale automatically. Support for multiple currencies with real-time conversion. Built for a global audience, not just one market.
