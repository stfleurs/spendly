How it works

Receet Pro uses the envelope method — the same approach that's helped millions of people take control of their money. Here's how it works in practice.

Create envelopes

Set up categories for groceries, gas, dining, fun, savings — whatever matters to you. Each envelope gets a budget.

Add your income

Link accounts or enter income manually. Receet Pro distributes it across your envelopes based on your rules.

Spend & track

Snap a receipt or enter transactions. Each purchase deducts from the right envelope automatically.

Know what's left

Check any envelope balance in one tap. Green means you're on track. Empty means stop spending there.

The difference? YNAB, EveryDollar, and Goodbudget all use some form of envelope budgeting. Receet Pro makes it instant — with receipt scanning that removes the manual work.
