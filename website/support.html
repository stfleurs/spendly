Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking the "Forgot Password" link on the login page of the application.

Can I export my data?

Yes, Receet Pro allows you to export your expense data in CSV and PDF formats directly from your dashboard.

Do you support multiple currencies?

Receet Pro automatically handles currency conversion based on real-time exchange rates for your convenience.

How secure is my financial data?

We use bank-level 256-bit encryption to ensure your data is secure both in transit and at rest.
